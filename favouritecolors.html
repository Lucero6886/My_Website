❓ Practice Questions

LEVEL: BASIC

  • "What color is this?"
  • "Is this red or green?"
  • "Do you like yellow?"

LEVEL: INTERMEDIATE

  • "What is your favorite color?"
  • "Can you see something blue?"
  • "What color is your shirt?"

LEVEL: ADVANCED

  • "Why do you like pink?"
  • "Tell me 3 things that are orange."
  • "If you mix red and white, what color do you get?"

📜 60-Min Lesson Plan

0-10'

Warm-up: Greet the student. Talk about mood colors. Check connection.

10-25'

Vocabulary: Intro colors + objects. Play "Repeat after me" with different speeds.

25-45'

Sentence Lab: Build full sentences. Focus on "The... is...". Use the Mic feature.

45-55'

Movement: Scavenger Hunt! Get the student to find real objects.

55-60'

Review: Award stars. Final goodbye. Assignment: Find 2 new colors at home.

Teaching Tip:

Always encourage full sentences. If they say "Blue", prompt with "It is...?" to get "It is blue!"
